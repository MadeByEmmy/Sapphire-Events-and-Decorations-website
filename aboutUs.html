Meet the Founder

Racheal is a seasoned event planner and party decorator, specializing in turning dreams into reality. she is the brain behind Sapphire Events and Decorations.
Welcome to Sapphire Events & Decorations, your go-to creative hub for crafting unforgettable moments since January 2023.
Based in the heart of Tallinn, Estonia, she pride herself on transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences. With a passion for detail and an eye for elegance, she specialize in curating personalized event decor that reflects your unique style and vision.
From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, let Sapphire Events and Decorations sprinkle a touch of Sapphire magic to make your special moments shine brighter.
Although we are based in Tallinn, we are available to travel anywhere in Estonia as well as anywhere in Europe.
