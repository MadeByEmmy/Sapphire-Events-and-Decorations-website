Frequently Asked Questions

What Services Do You Offer?

We plan, decorate and rent party Equipment. We offer luxury and basic decoration designs based on Clients requests. We also design and curate thoughtful gifts packages for special occasions.

What Exactly Does Sapphire Event Planning My Party Mean?

Planning a party is not a cake walk. It takes time, effort, and constant communication with different people and Vendors. The success of a party depends on the attention to details and how meticulously each plan is carried out.

Planning your party means :

1 - Sourcing for venues and vendors.

2- Contacting and negotiating with all your vendors to make sure we stay within your budget.

3- Taking that stress off you toward your big day, you don’t need to worry about every little detail. We have that covered, you just need to show up pretty to your event.

4- Coordinating all vendors to make sure your party is seamless.

5- Sourcing for and solely responsible for any physical activity on your big day. Including physical man power for moving all your heavy party equipments

These and so much more are what we do when we plan your party.

What Is The Average Cost Of Planning And Decorating?

We know this is your first question and how important it is to you. Budget and costs is a big factor but WE DO NOT HAVE A STANDARD PRICE. We create beautiful designs to suit your budget. Or help you improve on your budget and proffer our expertise designs to fit your financial budget.

We give different designs packages, from Luxury to basic. With each designs unique in its own way. The design also depends on the number of guests, and the complexity of the design, location, time and the type of event.

OUR PLANNING FEE is charged in percentages and this is determined by your wedding budget. During consultation, we will let you know how much exactly based on your budget.

OUR DECORATION COSTS - The average Sapphire Events wedding Decorations is a minimum of 500 Euros for a design. While the maximum depends on the clients and how luxurious you want it to be or not.

The number of guests, date, location is also a major determinant of our decorating costs. We also have packages for brides with lower budgets that would love the Sapphire Events touch.

Let us know if you have a WEDDING GUEST LIST OF 10/20 people, and we’d specially give you a cost based on your small number.

OTHER PARTY DESIGNS like birthdays, showers, christenings, engagements does not have a minimum, this is subject to clients desires and needs, wants and number of guests etc.
Please let us know the type of party you have so we can give you the minimal prices for these parties. It may be lesser than you’d expect, We would love to schedule a consultation with you.

Why Do I Need To Pay Deposit/Consultation Fee?

Planning a wedding or any party is tedious and time consuming at the same time. Consultation fee is our commitment charge for helping you with running around for your vendors.

Sometimes, we have to physically go to your venue locations to secure bookings, pay consultation fee for some of your proposed vendors, logistics etc before coming up with a budget for your wedding.

All this running cost entails money and Sapphire Event’s time. It is simply our service charge for taking the stress off you in contacting all your vendors, making bookings and coming up with a final wedding or party budget.

How Do You Take Payments? Can I Pay In Installment ?

WeWe have two basic payment plan.

1- In percentages. We give you a percentage plan to make payment before your party with a due date. It’s usually 70% upfront after our consultations and agreement to proceed and 30% a month to your Event date.

2- In Full. You can also make payments to us in full after consultation and agreement. This decision is optional and solely yours to take.

YES, You can pay Sapphire Events in installments.

We can draw up a payment plan for you to be pay leading up towards your event date, however a retainer of 40% must be paid after consultations and agreement to proceed. All payments must be completed a month before your event date.

The only exception to this is if you contacted us when your party is less than a month, then full payment must be made to secure the date.
