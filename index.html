We provide the following services for our clients

We offer a wide range of services to our clients, including full-service planning and coordination, event planning only, rentals of party accessories, party decorations only, and balloon garland styling. We can help you plan and decorate events such as weddings, engagements, birthdays, baby showers, bridal showers, proposals, baby christening and dedication, corporate events and official ceremonies, surprises, and funerals.
